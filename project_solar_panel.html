Solar Panel Project

This project collects and analyzes data from the solar panel array located on the exterior of the Princess Building. Currently, the data from the panels is collected and analyzed by a laptop running a piece of specialized software. This data is then displayed on one of the televisions located on the 4th floor of the Princess building. For more information, please visit Solar Panel Technology Corner.
