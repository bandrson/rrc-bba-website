Get Involved

Participation in the Bits and Bytes Association is proven to increase the skills required to perform in industry today. However, we understand that there are other commitments, assignments and the need for personal time. Joining us is optional and you are welcome to engage as much or as little as you want.

  • Come to our general meetings
  • Join the discussions on social media
  • Stop by our events (or plan one!)
  • Volunteer! - We always have new opportunities available
  • Celebrate finishing exams with us
  • Contribute to our website
